EIB Physical Fitness Assessment

The E3B Physical Fitness Assessment is designed to assess the physical fitness of Soldiers in a way that is relevant to the tasks that they will be required to perform in combat. The assessment is also designed to be challenging and to motivate Soldiers to maintain a high level of physical fitness.

The E3B Physical Fitness Assessment consists of seven events:

1. One-mile run
2. 30 "Dead-stop" push-ups
3. 100-meter sprint
4. 40lb sandbag lift
5. 50-meter farmer's carry
6. 50-meter movement lane
7. One-mile run
