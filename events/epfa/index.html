Phase 1:
EIB Physical Fitness Assessment

Phase 1 will occur on Test Day 1

The E3B Physical Fitness Assessment (EPFA) is designed to assess the physical fitness of Soldiers in a way that is relevant to the tasks that they will be required to perform in combat. The assessment is also designed to be challenging and to motivate Soldiers to maintain a high level of physical fitness.

The EPFA consists of seven events:

  1. One-mile run
  2. 30 "Dead-stop" push-ups
  3. 100-meter sprint
  4. 16x 40-lb sandbag lift
  5. 50-meter water can carry
  6. 50-meter movement lane (25m high crawl, 25m 3-5 second rush)
  7. One-mile run

The EPFA must be completed in 26:30 to pass.

The EPFA events are ran in order and continuously. No individual event is timed. This event is not retestable.

Uniform: OCPs, Kit (front and back plates), Helmet, Boots
