Phase 4:
12-Mile Foot March and Final Event

Phase 4 occurs on Test Day 5.

12-mile Foot March:

Candidates have 3 hours to complete a 12-mile Foot March.

Requirements: 35lbs dry ruck, TAPS, weapon.

This event is NOT retestable.


Final Event:

At the conclusion of the foot march, candidates will test the final event:

  1. 2.5 minutes to clear and disassemble their M4,
  2. 2.5 minutes to assemble and perform a functions check.

This event is NOT retestable.
