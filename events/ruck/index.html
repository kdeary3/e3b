Phase 4:
12-Mile Foot March
and Final Event

Phase 4 occurs on Test Day 5.

12-mile Foot March:

Candidates have 3 hours to complete a 12-mile Foot March.
Requirements: 35lbs dry ruck, TAPS, weapon.
The 12-mile foot march is a not a retestable event.

Final Event:

At the conclusion of the foot march, candidates will test the final event.

Candidates will have 2.5 minutes to clear and disassemble their M4, and 2.5 minutes to assemble and perform a functions check.

The final event is a not a retestable event.
