RTT 5: Employ/Recover an M18A1 Claymore Mine

Tasks:

  1. Employ an M18A1 Claymore Mine (In sequence).
  2. Recover an M18A1 Claymore Mine (In sequence).

Test Conditions:

    During daylight, the Candidate is given an M18A1 Claymore Mine (inert), an M57 firing device, an M40 test set, and firing wire with blasting cap (inert), all packed in an M7 bandoleer; an OP position at least 16 meters from the Claymore firing site; a sandbag and two wooden stakes.

Test Standards:

  1. Within 7 Minutes, Employs an M18A1 Claymore Mine (In sequence).
  2. Within 5 Minutes, Recovers an M18A1 Claymore Mine (In sequence).
