Phase 2: Land Navigation

Phase 2 occurs on Test Day 1.

Candidates have 3 hours to correctly identify 3/4 points and return to the start point. Candidates must pass a Day Lane and a Night Lane.

  • Candidates are not permitted to re-enter the course after reporting scorecard to EIB grader, even if candidate has returned before time has expired
  • Candidates must have their map and scorecard upon return.
  • Phones and other electrionic devices are unauthorized.
  • This event is NOT retestable.
