P8:
Resection and Military Maps

Task: Determine your location. Identify terrain features, colors, and contour lines. Identify topographic symbols.

Condition: You are a member of a team conducting tactical operations and have a requirement to determine your current location.

Standard: Determine the correct six-digit grid to your location including using either the map and compass method or the straight edge method of resection within five minutes. Identify five major, three minor, and two supplementary terrain features, what the six basic colors represent, and the three types of contour lines within five minutes with 100% accuracy. Correctly identify 22 topographic symbols within ten minutes.
