Phase 3:
Individual Testing Stations

Phase 3 will occur on Test Days 2-4.

Note: Each individual station is retestable. However, 2 NO-GOs at a single station will result in disqualification. More than 3 NO-GOs in total will result in disqualification.

Weapons Tasks:
Candidates will be tested on unload, clear, disassemble, assemble, perform a functions check, conduct immediate action, and/or employ the following:

  1. M4 Carbine/Rifle
  2. M320
  3. M9 or M17/M18 Pistol
  4. M500-series Shotgun
  5. M249
  6. M240
  7. M67 Hand Grenades
  8. M18A1 Claymore Mine
  9. M98 Javelin
  10. M2 .50 Caliber Machine Gun
  11. MK19 Heavy Grenade Launcher
  12. AT4 Anti-Tank Weapons (M3, 84-mm Recoilless Rifle)

Medical Tasks:

  1. Request MEDEVAC
  2. Provide Care under Fire and Move a Casualty
  3. Perform First Aid to Restore Breathing and/or Pulse
  4. Evaluate a Casualty for a Heat Injury
  5. Control Bleeding
  6. Evaluate and Treat a Casualty for a Spinal Injury and Shock
  7. Apply an Occlusive Dressing and Perform a Needle Chest Decompression
  8. Perform First Aid for an Open Head Wound in a CBRNE Environment
  9. Treat an Open Abdominal Wound and Eye Injuries
  10. Treat a Fracture and a Burn

Patrol Lane Tasks:

  1. Call Indirect Fire and Adjust Indirect Fire
  2. Move under Direct Fire
  3. Tactical Handheld Radio (SINGARS)
  4. Defense Advanced GPS Receiver (DAGR) Operations
  5. Camouflage and Visual Signaling Techniques
  6. Range Card
  7. Chemical and Biological Operations. Assume MOPP Level 3
  8. Resection and Military Maps
  9. Operate PVS-14 Monocular Night Vision Device
  10. Transmit a Spot Report with a Tactical Man Pack Radio
